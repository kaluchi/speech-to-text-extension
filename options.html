Voice Input Settings

You can get an API key by registering at elevenlabs.io

Select the recognition language to improve accuracy.

When enabled, pauses, laughter, and other audio events will be marked in the recognized text.

Select the level of timestamp detail in the transcription.

Enable to identify who is speaking and when in the recording.

Specify the maximum number of speakers (1 to 32). Leave empty for auto-detection.

Add keywords and their weights (-10 to 10) to improve recognition.

Choose a specific microphone to speed up recording initialization. The list will update when settings are opened.

When enabled, the recording will be played back while being sent to the server.

When enabled, a color indicator will be displayed during recording (yellow during initialization, green during recording).
